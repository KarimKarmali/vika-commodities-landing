Global Trading Excellence in Sustainable Feedstocks

Part of the Vika Group, scaling sustainable impact worldwide

Vika Commodities is a global trading company specialized in advanced feedstocks for the production of renewable fuels, including HVO (Hydrotreated Vegetable Oil) and SAF (Sustainable Aviation Fuel). We source and supply a wide portfolio of sustainable raw materials — such as UCO (Used Cooking Oil), animal fats, and other waste- and residue-based inputs — to biorefineries and fuel producers around the world.

Part of the Vika Group, our operations are rooted in circular economy principles and powered by smart logistics, advanced traceability systems, and deep market knowledge. From sourcing to delivery, we ensure integrity, compliance, and high-quality standards at every step.

With strategic presence in Europe, South-East Asia, the Middle East, and North Africa, we bridge global supply chains and connect reliable producers with demanding industrial clients. Through trusted partnerships and a strong on-the-ground network, we respond with agility to evolving regulatory and market dynamics.

"At Vika Commodities, we're not just moving volumes — we're scaling sustainable impact."
